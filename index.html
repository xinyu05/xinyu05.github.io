About

I'm Xinyu Xiao, born and raised in Xiamen, China with great zeal for exploring the world, I have been crafting digital experiences in both China and Germany. As a self-motivated individual, I keep pursuing cutting-edge design techniques while reflecting on the fundamental human nature. I appreciate designs that demonstrate simplicity, clarity and profoundness.

In 2015, I came to Germany to study Master program integrated design process at Coburg University of Applied Sciences focusing interaction design. The days I spent with group members, playing with different design methods and walking in the snow will be invaluable treasure I carry along.

Before, I studied Industrial design in Beijing, where I started my journey to be a designer. I learned to find problem, do research, create solutions, play the guitar, travel around, and grew to be who I am.

I enjoy designing for cross-platform services and applications to connect the virtual and physical world. I am passionate about solving real problems and improving people’s daily life. I've designed products from conception to launch for both mobile and web.

In my spare time you can find me sketching, swimming, playing piano, attending Meetup, trying new apps and forcing them on friends and family.
