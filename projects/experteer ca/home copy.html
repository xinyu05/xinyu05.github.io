Overview

The new Experteer mobile experience steps away from a mere job board. It's different from competitors by providing tailored content that is valuable for your next career step. Instead of asking you to operate a search engine, we do it for you by knowing your wishes and needs.



Research & Ideas

First it took me sometime to understood the business model of Experteer and the vision of new mobile experience, we start with competitor analysis and research to explore ideas.



Market Position

Experteer has a unique market position as the premium career and recruitment service in 10 European markets and the US. Experteer is built upon two pillars: The B2C side which is compiled of 6 million candidates and 250.000 executive positions, and the B2B side, consisting of 10.000 headhunters and 8.000 corporate recruiters, who have all been approved by Experteer. Experteer only advertises executive positions and is entirely confidential for our candidates, if they want it to be. Candidates cannot see each other’s profiles and communication between candidates and headhunters is initiated by the headhunters via contact request.
