Overview

This is a concept developed in course Interaction Design Basic directed by Prof.Lutz. When cooking you have to wash a lot and then dry your hands, which consumes a lot of time and is therefore not practical. Our idea is to develop an interactive hand dryer in the kitchen for ordinary family. In the kitchen you always need a dryer, it is convenient that the dryer with the faucet connects together and with a wave of the hand you can also control the heat intensity and manual ventilation.
