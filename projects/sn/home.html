Hassle-Free Service For Mini Drivers, Mini Has Created Servicenow, A Reliable, Personal, Valued And Non-Intrusive Service Companion - Offering A Seamless And Trustworthy Service Experience.


Reponsibility

We built a assle-Free Service For Mini Drivers called ServiceNow. We designed and implemented the following functionalities for this app: event related notification, current & last location , vehicle status and safty & security.


— Design —




EVENT RELATED NOTIFICATION

Includes basically all condition based service (CBS) noti cations such as: engine oil service due, oil service with micro lter, air lter and spark plug change due, brake uid service, front and rear brake pads, vehicle check, MOT service due. Furthermore it includes the display of the most critical warning lights.




CURRENT & LAST LOCATION

Including an anti theft warning if the car has moved abruptly plus the feature „Find my car“. If supported in the future, this shall even work inside a multi- storey car park.





VEHICLE STATUS

Clusters service-related information like last maintenance, end of warranty, next MOT and TLC status. And most importantly: are the car doors and windows open or closed?





BOOKING

Includes the full booking cycle: from creating an appointment, asking for a call-back, requesting pick-up and a courtesy car through to a full receipt and service log of what has been done.





SERVICE HISTORY

Lists all services your MINI has gone through for future reference.



SAFETY & SECURITY

Breakdown assistant, Emergency call, Accident checklist, Contextual intelligent messages focused on safety.

SERVICE EXTENDEDD

Troubleshoot, Service companion, Expert forum.


CAR ACCESSOIRES

Accessory-con gurator.
